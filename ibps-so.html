📌 Selection Process

  1. Preliminary Exam (Objective MCQ) – Qualifying in nature.
  2. Mains Exam (Professional Knowledge + Descriptive Test for some posts).
  3. Interview (Final selection based on Mains + Interview marks).

📌 Exam Pattern

Prelims Exam (Common for all posts except Rajbhasha Adhikari)

Section Questions Marks Duration
English 50 25 40 mins
Reasoning 50 50 40 mins
General Awareness (Banking) 50 50 40 mins

Rajbhasha Adhikari has Hindi instead of English

Mains Exam (Professional Knowledge)

  • IT Officer, AFO, Law Officer, etc.: 60 Qs (60 Marks, 45 mins).

📌 Salary & Benefits
